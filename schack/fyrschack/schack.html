SCHACKSPEL

Description:

This is an unfinished chess game written in javascript The idea was to make a 4 player chess that could be played locally as I could not find any such games available online. However it turns out that figuring out wether or not a piece is allowed to move is complex. Don't want to allow movement that would cause the player to put them selves in check. The complexity increases way too much when four players are involved. I figured that out much too late. That's why it's unfinished. The base mechanics work fine though. The pieces have their move-sets and can be taken etc.. no win screen though. It's kind of like playing chess away from the keyboard.
